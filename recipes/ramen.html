Tsukemen

Description:

Dipping ramen. Think ramen, but with the noodles separated from the soup.

Just buy it from a store. Too hard to make.

IMPORTANT: Do not pour the soup onto the noodles or vice versa.

Ingredients:

  • Money

How to eat:

  1. Pick up noodles.
  2. Dip in soup.
  3. Eat. (It is ok to drink the soup by itself or with the noodles.)
